# Disclaimer

GlicoCalc is an educational carb-planning tool intended to help children with type 1 diabetes and their caregivers estimate food portions for a chosen carbohydrate target.

It is not a medical device, and it does not provide:

- medical advice
- diagnosis
- treatment recommendations
- insulin dosing guidance
- emergency support

Nutrition values may be incomplete, estimated, or entered incorrectly. Food labels, recipes, and preparation methods can change the actual carbohydrate content of a meal.

Anyone using this app remains responsible for verifying nutrition data and making care decisions with an appropriate clinician, caregiver, or established treatment plan.

If a user feels unwell or may be experiencing hypoglycemia, hyperglycemia, or another medical emergency, they should follow their prescribed care plan and seek urgent medical help where appropriate.
